This thing is a selenium WhatsApp new message checker. Program need the
information of Telegram bot add Telegram id to work. Also to start it you 
have to scan qr code(there were no way to roundabout it, P.S. I tried!!).
You can to mute chats by puting their names in the "main.first_dict" and 
change interval of massage sending for you in Telegram.

Bot walk across chats and if there any new message, it will send it for you
in Telegram, pretty simple and useful of cource!
